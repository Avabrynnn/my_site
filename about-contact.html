About

I am a multidisciplinary artist currently studying at The New School in New York City. My work explores themes of memory, identity, and the natural world through various media including painting, digital art, and installation.

I draw inspiration from my surroundings and personal experiences, seeking to create a dialogue between traditional art practices and contemporary visual language.
