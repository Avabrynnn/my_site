DETAILED ARTWORKS

Chromatic Dreams (2024)

Medium: Digital illustration

Year: 2024

Description: This piece explores the intersection of consciousness and technology through vibrant color fields and abstract forms. The digital medium allows for a unique exploration of light and movement that wouldn't be possible in traditional media.

Urban Flow (2023)

Medium: Digital composition

Year: 2023

Description: A meditation on city life and movement through urban spaces. This work captures the energy and rhythm of metropolitan environments through abstracted architectural elements and dynamic lines.

Fragments (2023)

Medium: Mixed media installation

Year: 2023

Description: An assemblage of found objects and painted elements that comments on consumption and waste in modern society. The piece invites viewers to reconsider the value and lifecycle of everyday materials.

Memory Palace (2023)

Medium: Photography and mixed media

Year: 2023

Description: An exploration of personal history and collective memory through layered photographic images and handwritten text. This work examines how memories are preserved, altered, and reconstructed over time.

Spring Awakening (2024)

Medium: Acrylic on canvas

Year: 2024

Description: A vibrant exploration of color and organic forms inspired by seasonal change. This painting captures the energy and renewal of spring through gestural brushwork and a harmonious color palette.
