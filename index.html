About Ava Hyland

Biography

Ava Hyland is a contemporary artist based in New York City, specializing in mixed media and digital art. Currently studying at The New School, Ava's work explores themes of identity, technology, and environmental consciousness.

With a background in traditional painting techniques and a growing interest in digital media, Ava creates pieces that bridge the gap between conventional art forms and new technological possibilities.

Education

  • BFA in Fine Arts, The New School, New York (Expected 2026)
  • Summer Intensive Program, School of Visual Arts, 2023

Selected Exhibitions

  • 2024: "Digital Landscapes," Student Gallery, The New School
  • 2023: "Emerging Artists Showcase," Community Arts Center
  • 2022: "First Light," Group Exhibition, Local Gallery
