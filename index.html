AVA HYLAND

I am a contemporary artist based in New York City, specializing in mixed media and digital art. Currently studying at The New School, my work explores themes of identity, technology, and environmental consciousness.

With a background in traditional painting techniques and a growing interest in digital media, I create pieces that bridge the gap between conventional art forms and new technological possibilities.
